## 👨‍💻 Who I Am

I'm a **product-minded software engineer** who loves building things that feel effortless to use — and powerful underneath.

To me, great engineering isn't just about writing code. It's about **clarity, ownership, and craft**: turning messy ideas into systems that are reliable, scalable, and easy to evolve. I think from the user's perspective first, then design the backend architecture that makes the experience smooth, fast, and consistent.

> **"Build for people first — the tech will follow."**

The best products aren't remembered for their complexity — they're remembered for how they made someone feel: confident, supported, and in control.
